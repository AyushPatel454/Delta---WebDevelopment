Justify Content , Align Items & Align Content

Justify-Content: Tells how the browser distributed space between and around content items along the main-axis.

Align-Items: Distributed our items along the cross axis.

Align-Content: It sets the distribution of space between and around content items along a flexbox's cross-axis. (We can use this instead of Align-items because it hase Justify-content property also so we can apply in both main axis & cross axis. )

Align-Slef: Aligns an item along the cross axis. (Align Self is same as Align Items but Align self apply on individual items & Align Items apply on Group of items. Align Self > Align Items )
